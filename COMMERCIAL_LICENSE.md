## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

You must obtain a commercial license if:

1. **SaaS & Cloud Services:** You wish to provide AInalyst as a hosted service (SaaS)
   or part of a cloud platform without making your own source code public under the AGPL.

2. **Proprietary Integration:** You want to integrate AInalyst code into a closed-source
   commercial product.

3. **Custom GUIs:** You are building a **paid or commercial** graphical user interface
   on top of AInalyst and do not wish to release your UI code under the AGPL.

4. **Enterprise Requirements:** Your organization requires a traditional commercial
   agreement with warranties, indemnification, or dedicated support.
